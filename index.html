I am a researcher (pos doc) of National Institute of Informatics, where locates in Tokyo, Japan. I hold a Ph.D (2020) in NII, SOKENDAI , Tokyo, Japan and M.Sc. (2017) in School of Computer Science and Technology from HIT, China.

My primary research interests include 1) Metric Learning (currently) and Representation Learning (Ph.D phrase) for Multimodal Information Retrieval. 2) Name Entity Recognition & Relation Extraction in Natural Language Processing (M.Sc phrase).
